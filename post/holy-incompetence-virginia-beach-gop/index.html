Holy Incompetence, Virginia Beach GOP!

Nov. 1, 2018

We are 5 days away from the 2018 midterm elections, and the Republican Party of Virginia Beach doesn't have a voter guide anywhere on their website. How is that even possible? It is literally written in the party's (poorly worded) bylaws that anyone who wishes to join the RPVB must declare their support for all Republican candidates, yet your web admin couldn't be bothered to put a list of these people on your website.

Telling people who you support is the most day-one, obvious aspect of running a political campaign I can possibly imagine. I can't believe this has to be said.
